To pay them per ... task! Yeah, that's right! I mean think about it: you already have a task system in place, don't you? Whether it's Pivotal Tracker, Jira or Teambox.

So why don't you do it? Because it'd be a lot of hassle. I know. But that's where PPT comes in! We nailed that one and in fact I'm happy to tell you that this will not only SKYROCKET motivation amongst your dev team, it will also significantly REDUCE paperwork.

"But I'll have to change my issue tracker for your solution, right?"
"Wrong! You won't have to change a damn thing! Keep whatever systems you are using and just plug in this little component which will work its ass off without you even noticing it's there!"

How does it work? First register and enable the plugin. You will receive a step-by-step explanation in the email you'll receive upon registration. You create a story in issue tracker and you put a price tag into the description.

From the pool of available stories, each developer can select whichever story gives him the best ROI based on his unique skills and experience.

Once he's done, he changes the status to "finished". As usual. Then you'll get a notification about it, again, as usual and you review it. If you're happy with the work, you accept the story.

At that point, you'll be issued with an invoice on behalf of your developer and money for the story will be sent to his account immediately.

Developers will to love it, because they get the money in real time. That's a huge deal for freelancers, to be sure that they get paid and to get paid often.
