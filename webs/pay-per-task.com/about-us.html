Let me tell you a story ...

PayPerTask was born of our frustration with cashflow, time sheets, unfair pay.

We're scratching our own itch. Of course when PPT will take off, we'll be disgustingly rich and we'll be off shagging hot girls in the Caribean, but if that wouldn't happen, this is what we would want to be using.

It hasn't been designed from the white board for companies, it was created for how we'd like to work.
